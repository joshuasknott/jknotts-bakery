About Us

I’m a student baker with a serious sweet tooth. J. Knott’s Bakery is my passion project—sharing indulgent, handcrafted bakes that bring comfort and joy. Whether it’s a gooey brownie or a nostalgic sprinkle cake, I believe everyone deserves a treat that warms the soul.
